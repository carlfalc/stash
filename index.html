FAQ

How does the AI recognition work?

Our computer vision AI can identify thousands of household items from a simple photo. It learns your specific items over time and gets more accurate with use.

What about privacy and data?

Your inventory data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell personal information or share your belongings data with third parties.

Can I export for insurance?

Yes! Pro plans include one-click export to PDF with photos, purchase dates, and estimated values formatted for insurance companies.

How does duplicate detection work?

When shopping online, our browser extension checks your Stash and alerts you if you already own similar items. Works with Amazon, Home Depot, and 50+ retailers.
